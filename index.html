Who I am?

Junior web dev - passionately curious

I am a junior developer and curious about all new things. I did my first HTML webpage at upper secodary school where I created a fansite for The X-Files. I wrote reviews about the episodes I watched and I was a passionate X-Files fan. This was in the 90's, in the era when connecting to internet meant that nobody else could call you at the same time.

Back to 2021 after several years in IT-Support I decided it's time for change and to face new challenges. I wanted to start a new career in an area which I could develop myself further and learn new things. I wanted to be able to build things. To be able to say instead of asking "Have you tried turning it off and on again?" - "I built it!" So I did a coding bootcamp at WBS coding school and built my first fullstack app for the L-Night group using the MERN stack.

Currently I am learning TS and deepening my knowledge in JS and in the MERN stack. I have made also my first requests and mutations using GRAPHQL.
